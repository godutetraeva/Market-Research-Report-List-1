<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The 3D Printed Jewelry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SLA Technology</li><li>SLS Technology</li><li>DLP Technology</li><li>FDM Technology</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D printed jewelry market is categorized into several technology types. SLA (Stereolithography) technology offers high precision and a smooth finish, ideal for intricate designs. SLS (Selective Laser Sintering) uses powdered materials, allowing for durable and complex structures, but may have a rougher texture. DLP (Digital Light Processing) provides speed and fine detail through light-sensitive resins. FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) is cost-effective but generally produces lower-quality results. Other technologies include binder jetting and PolyJet, expanding design possibilities and material options.</p></p>
