<p><strong>The Absorbent Dressings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Adherent</li><li>Adherent</li><li>Low-Adherent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Absorbent dressings are categorized into non-adherent, adherent, and low-adherent types based on their ability to stick to wounds. Non-adherent dressings prevent sticking to the wound surface, making them suitable for delicate or healing tissues. Adherent dressings bond tightly to the wound, providing stable coverage but may cause discomfort during removal. Low-adherent dressings offer moderate adherence, striking a balance between secure attachment and gentle removal, making them ideal for various wound management scenarios, ensuring effective absorption and comfort.</p></p>
