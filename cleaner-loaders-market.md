<p><p>The cleaner-loaders market is categorized into three main types: self-propelled, trailed, and mounted. Self-propelled cleaner-loaders are independent machines with their own power source, allowing for enhanced mobility and efficiency in various terrains. Trailed cleaner-loaders attach to a towing vehicle, offering flexibility in usage and transportation. Mounted cleaner-loaders are designed to be affixed directly onto tractors or other machinery, providing a compact solution that maximizes existing equipment. Each type serves distinct operational needs in agricultural and industrial applications.</p></p>
