<p><p>The continuous mixer market features two primary types: single arm and double arm continuous mixers. Single arm continuous mixers utilize one mixing element that blends materials efficiently, ideal for simpler formulations and smaller production volumes. In contrast, double arm continuous mixers consist of two interlocking arms that provide more thorough mixing, making them suitable for complex formulations and larger production needs. Both types are designed for seamless, uninterrupted processing, enhancing productivity in industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
