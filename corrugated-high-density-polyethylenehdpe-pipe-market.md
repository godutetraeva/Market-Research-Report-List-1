<p><p>The Corrugated High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) pipe market primarily consists of two types: Single-Wall and Double Wall Corrugated HDPE pipes. Single-Wall Corrugated HDPE pipes are characterized by their lightweight and flexibility, making them suitable for drainage and irrigation applications. In contrast, Double Wall Corrugated HDPE pipes feature an inner smooth wall and an outer corrugated wall, providing enhanced structural strength and durability, ideal for sewer and stormwater systems. Both types are valued for their resistance to corrosion and ease of installation.</p></p>
