<p><p>The CT machine market comprises three main types: Non-Spiral Scan CT, Single-Slice Spiral Scan CT, and Multi-Slice Spiral Scan CT. Non-Spiral Scan CT offers basic imaging capabilities, primarily used for simple diagnostic needs. Single-Slice Spiral Scan CT enhances image acquisition speed and efficiency, providing a single slice per rotation for improved diagnostics. Multi-Slice Spiral Scan CT, the most advanced, captures multiple slices simultaneously, allowing for high-resolution images and quicker scans, accommodating complex diagnostic requirements and expanding clinical applications.</p></p>
