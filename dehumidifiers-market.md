<p><p>The dehumidifier market is primarily divided into two types: portable and non-portable dehumidifiers. Portable dehumidifiers are compact and easily movable, designed for personal use in specific rooms or smaller spaces, making them ideal for temporary humidity control. Non-portable dehumidifiers, on the other hand, are larger, typically installed in basements or whole-home systems, and are designed for continuous operation in larger areas. Both types effectively reduce humidity but serve different user needs and environments.</p></p>
