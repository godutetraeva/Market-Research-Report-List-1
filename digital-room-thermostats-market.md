<p><strong>The Digital Room Thermostats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Millivolt Thermostats</li><li>24 Volt Thermostats</li><li>Line Voltage Thermostats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital room thermostats market is segmented into three main types: millivolt thermostats, 24-volt thermostats, and line voltage thermostats. Millivolt thermostats are used in systems that operate on low voltage, commonly found in gas-fired systems. 24-volt thermostats are standard in central heating and cooling systems, providing precise temperature control. Line voltage thermostats, typically used in electric heating systems, operate on higher voltage directly from the electrical supply. Each type serves distinct heating and cooling needs based on system compatibility.</p></p>
