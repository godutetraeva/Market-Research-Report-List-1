<p><p>Disaster Recovery-as-a-Service (DRaaS) is a cloud-based service that enables organizations to back up their data and IT infrastructure offsite, ensuring business continuity in case of disasters. The market can be categorized into two main types: Premises-to-Cloud, where data and applications are backed up from on-premises environments to cloud storage; and Cloud-to-Cloud, which involves replicating data across different cloud providers for enhanced redundancy and resilience. Both models aim to reduce downtime and streamline recovery processes.</p></p>
