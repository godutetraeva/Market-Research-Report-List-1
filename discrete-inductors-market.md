<p><strong>The Discrete Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wirewound Coils</li><li>Deposited Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The discrete inductors market primarily includes wirewound coils and deposited coils. Wirewound coils are constructed by winding conductive wire around a core, offering high inductance and efficiency, making them ideal for applications requiring higher power handling. In contrast, deposited coils, created using thin-film or thick-film techniques, provide a compact design with lower inductance values, suitable for high-frequency applications like RF circuits. Both types cater to diverse industries, including consumer electronics, automotive, and telecommunications, each with specific performance requirements.</p></p>
