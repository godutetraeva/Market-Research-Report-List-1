<p><p>The enamelled aluminium wire market is primarily categorized into two types: rectangular and round enamelled aluminium wires. Rectangular enamelled aluminium wire features a flat shape, offering higher surface area for efficient heat dissipation and is often used in transformers and inductors. In contrast, round enamelled aluminium wire is cylindrical, providing superior electrical conductivity and flexibility, making it suitable for a wide range of applications, including motors and generators. Both types are essential for industries requiring lightweight and durable wiring solutions.</p></p>
