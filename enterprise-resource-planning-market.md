<p><p>Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premise ERP and cloud-based ERP. On-premise ERP involves software installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based ERP is hosted on remote servers, offering flexible access, scalability, and lower initial costs while being maintained by the vendor. Businesses choose between these options based on their specific needs, budget, and IT capabilities.</p></p>
