<p><p>Exhaust sensors play a crucial role in automotive emissions control and performance. Exhaust temperature and pressure sensors monitor the exhaust system's heat and pressure for optimal engine performance. Oxygen (O2) sensors measure oxygen levels in exhaust gases to adjust fuel mixtures. Nitrogen oxide (NOx) sensors detect harmful NOx emissions for compliance with regulations. Manifold Absolute Pressure (MAP) and Mass Air Flow (MAF) sensors measure air intake for precise engine tuning. Other sensors may include particulate matter sensors, enhancing emissions accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
