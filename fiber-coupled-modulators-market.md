<p><p>The Fiber-coupled Modulators market is primarily divided into two types: Single-Mode (SM) Fiber and Super-Mode (SMPM) Fiber. SM Fiber modulators are designed for applications requiring high precision and are commonly used in telecommunications and high-speed data transmission. SMPM Fiber modulators, on the other hand, offer enhanced performance by supporting multiple modes, making them suitable for applications that demand higher power and improved signal integrity. Both types cater to different needs in optical communication and photonic systems.</p></p>
