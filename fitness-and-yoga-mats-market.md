<p><p>The fitness and yoga mats market features various materials catering to diverse user preferences and needs. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) offers eco-friendliness and lightweight properties. Natural rubber is favored for its grip and sustainability. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is cost-effective but less eco-friendly. Other materials may include jute or cotton blends, catering to niche markets and preferences.</p></p>
