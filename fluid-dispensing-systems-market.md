<p><p>Fluid dispensing systems encompass various types, including valves, pumps, and dispensers, each serving unique functions in managing fluid application. Valves control the flow of liquids or gases, ensuring precise delivery. Pumps are responsible for moving fluids from one location to another, often providing varying flow rates and pressure levels. Dispensers, on the other hand, provide direct dispensing of fluids in controlled amounts, frequently utilized in industries like healthcare, automotive, and food packaging, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in applications.</p></p>
