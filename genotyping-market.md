<p><p>The genotyping market encompasses various technologies used for DNA analysis. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies specific DNA sequences, making it easier to study genetic variations. Sequencing determines the precise order of nucleotides in DNA, enhancing understanding of genetic makeup. Microarrays enable simultaneous analysis of multiple genes, allowing for high-throughput screening. Electrophoresis separates DNA fragments by size, facilitating visualization and comparison. MALDI-TOF employs mass spectrometry for rapid and accurate identification of genetic material, contributing to advanced genotyping applications.</p></p>
