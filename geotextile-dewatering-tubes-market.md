<p><p>The geotextile dewatering tubes market is divided into two main types: woven and nonwoven geotextile dewatering tubes. Woven geotextile tubes are made from interlaced fibers, providing high tensile strength and durability, making them ideal for applications requiring significant load-bearing capacity. Nonwoven geotextile tubes, on the other hand, consist of bonded fibers that offer excellent filtration and drainage properties, making them suitable for sediment control and water management. Each type serves distinct applications, addressing various environmental and construction-related needs.</p></p>
