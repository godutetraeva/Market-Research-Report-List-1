<p><p>Green roofs and walls are categorized into two main market types: extensive and intensive. Extensive green roofs feature a shallow substrate layer supporting drought-resistant plants, requiring minimal maintenance and irrigation, making them ideal for urban areas with lower load-bearing capacity. In contrast, intensive green roofs and walls have deeper soil layers that support a wider variety of plants, including shrubs and trees, necessitating more maintenance and irrigation, but offering greater biodiversity and aesthetic benefits. These systems contribute to sustainability and urban ecology.</p></p>
