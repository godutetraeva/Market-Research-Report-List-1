<p><ul><li>Analog Output</li><li>Digital Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic brake pressure sensor market is divided into two main types based on output: analog output and digital output. </p><p>Analog output sensors provide a continuous voltage or current signal proportional to brake pressure, allowing for real-time monitoring and responsiveness in braking systems. </p><p>Digital output sensors, on the other hand, deliver discrete signals indicating pressure levels, benefiting modern vehicle systems through enhanced integration with electronic control units for improved accuracy and diagnostics. Both types are crucial for vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
