<p><p>The light business jet market is primarily divided into two categories: Light Jets and Very Light Jets (VLJs). Light jets typically offer a larger capacity, accommodating up to 8 passengers and reaching longer ranges, making them suitable for short to medium-haul flights. In contrast, VLJs are smaller, designed for 4-6 passengers, and are optimized for shorter trips, often with lower operating costs and faster turnarounds. Both types serve the growing demand for efficient and flexible air travel in the business sector.</p></p>
