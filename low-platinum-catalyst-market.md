<p><p>The Low Platinum Catalyst market encompasses three main types: Pt-based Alloy Catalysts, which combine platinum with other metals to enhance catalytic activity and reduce platinum usage; Pt-based Core Shell Catalysts, featuring a platinum shell around a less expensive core to maximize efficiency while minimizing costs; and Pt Single Atom Catalysts, where individual platinum atoms are dispersed on supports, offering exceptional catalytic properties and resource efficiency. These innovations aim to improve performance while addressing cost and resource sustainability in various applications.</p></p>
