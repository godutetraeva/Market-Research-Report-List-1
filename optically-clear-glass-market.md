<p><p>The Optically Clear Glass market primarily consists of Float Glass and Rolled Glass. Float Glass is produced by floating molten glass on a bed of molten tin, resulting in a smooth, uniform surface ideal for windows and displays. Rolled Glass, on the other hand, is crafted by rolling molten glass to achieve specific thicknesses and textures, often used in architectural and decorative applications. Both types offer superior clarity and optical properties, serving diverse industries including construction, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
