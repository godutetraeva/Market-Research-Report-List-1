<p><p>The OTC gastrointestinal products market includes H2 receptor antagonists and proton pump inhibitors (PPIs), both aimed at relieving symptoms of acid-related disorders. H2 receptor antagonists reduce stomach acid production by blocking histamine receptors, providing quick relief for conditions like heartburn and gastritis. In contrast, PPIs inhibit the proton pump in stomach cells, offering prolonged acid suppression and healing for ulcers and gastroesophageal reflux disease. Both categories cater to consumers seeking effective over-the-counter solutions for gastrointestinal discomfort.</p></p>
