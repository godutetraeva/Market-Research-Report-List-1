<p><p>The outdoor circuit breaker market is segmented into three main types: low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage. Low voltage circuit breakers are designed for applications up to 1 kV and are commonly used in residential and commercial settings. Medium voltage breakers, typically operating between 1 kV and 36 kV, serve industrial and utility applications. High voltage circuit breakers, exceeding 36 kV, are essential for power transmission and distribution systems, ensuring reliable operation in high-demand environments. Each type plays a crucial role in electrical safety and system reliability.</p></p>
