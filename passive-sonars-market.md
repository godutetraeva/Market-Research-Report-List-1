<p><p>The passive sonar market is categorized into three main frequency types: low frequency, medium frequency, and high frequency. Low-frequency sonar operates below 1 kHz, ideal for long-range detection and tracking of submarines. Medium-frequency sonar, ranging from 1 to 10 kHz, balances range and resolution, suitable for anti-submarine warfare and surface vessel detection. High-frequency sonar, above 10 kHz, offers improved resolution for detecting smaller objects and conducting detailed underwater surveys, making it useful for various military and commercial applications.</p></p>
