<p><ul><li>Microfiltration (MF)</li><li>Ultrafiltration (UF)</li><li>Reverse Osmosis(RO)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymer separation membrane market encompasses various types, including microfiltration (MF), ultrafiltration (UF), and reverse osmosis (RO). MF operates at larger pore sizes, effectively removing suspended solids and bacteria. UF has smaller pores, allowing it to separate macromolecules and larger organic compounds. RO utilizes a semi-permeable membrane to eliminate salts and dissolved solids, providing high-purity water. Each type serves distinct applications across industries such as water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals, driven by increasing demand for efficient separation technologies.</p></p>
