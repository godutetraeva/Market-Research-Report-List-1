<p><p>Power and energy loggers are essential tools for monitoring electrical consumption in different installation types. Single-phase installations involve a single alternating current waveform, commonly used in residential settings. Split-phase installations feature two phases, typically found in North American homes for better efficiency and higher voltage options. Three-phase installations, prevalent in industrial and commercial applications, utilize three alternating currents for balanced load distribution, enhanced performance, and reduced energy losses. Each type addresses specific monitoring needs based on electrical configurations.</p></p>
