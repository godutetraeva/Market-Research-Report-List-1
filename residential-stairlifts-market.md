<p><p>The residential stairlift market comprises primarily two types: straight stairlifts and curved stairlifts. Straight stairlifts are designed for straightforward, linear staircases, making them cost-effective and easy to install. In contrast, curved stairlifts are custom-built to accommodate the unique contours and angles of curved or winding staircases, offering a tailored solution for more complex home layouts. Both types enhance mobility and accessibility within residences, ensuring safety and convenience for individuals with mobility challenges.</p></p>
