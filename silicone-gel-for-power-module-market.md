<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone gel for power modules is available in two primary types: single-component and two-component formulations. Single-component gels are ready to use and cure upon exposure to moisture, offering ease of application and reduced handling time. In contrast, two-component gels require mixing a base and a curing agent before application, allowing for customizable properties like viscosity and cure speed. Both types provide excellent thermal conductivity and dielectric strength, making them ideal for enhancing the reliability and performance of power modules.</p></p>
