<p><strong>The Soil Moisture Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive Soil Moisture Sensor</li><li>Capacitive Soil Moisture Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soil moisture sensor market is primarily divided into two types: resistive and capacitive sensors. Resistive soil moisture sensors measure moisture by detecting changes in electrical resistance between two probes inserted into the soil; their simplicity and low cost make them popular but can be prone to corrosion. Capacitive soil moisture sensors, on the other hand, measure moisture levels by assessing changes in capacitance, offering greater durability and accuracy, making them suitable for long-term agricultural and environmental monitoring applications.</p></p>
