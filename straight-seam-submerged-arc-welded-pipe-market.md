<p><p>The straight seam submerged arc welded (SSAW) pipe market includes several manufacturing processes, primarily the JCO process and UOE process. The JCO process involves forming pipes by sequentially bending flat sheets into a "J," then a "C," and finally an "O" shape before welding. The UOE process, on the other hand, utilizes a press to create a "U" shape and then an "O" shape for welding. Other methods may include helical and spiral welding, catering to diverse applications and specifications in industries like oil and gas, construction, and infrastructure.</p></p>
