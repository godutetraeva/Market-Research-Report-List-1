<p><p>The structural assembly adhesives market consists primarily of two types: paste adhesives and tape adhesives. Tape adhesives, on the other hand, offer convenience and ease of use, available in various forms like double-sided and foam tapes. They are widely used in electronics and packaging, where quick assembly and flexibility are essential. Both types cater to diverse bonding needs across industries.</p></p>
