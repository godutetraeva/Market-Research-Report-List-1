<p><strong>The Welding Inspection Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Destructive Testing</li><li>Non-destructive Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Welding Inspection Service Market is divided into two main types: Destructive Testing (DT) and Non-destructive Testing (NDT). Destructive Testing involves methods that result in the alteration or failure of the welded component, such as tensile testing or impact testing, to evaluate its strength and integrity. In contrast, Non-destructive Testing allows for the assessment of weld quality without damaging the material, using techniques like ultrasonic, radiographic, or magnetic particle testing, making it essential for ensuring safety and reliability in structures.</p></p>
